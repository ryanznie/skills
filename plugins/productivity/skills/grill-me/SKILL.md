# Grill Me

Use this skill when the user wants a plan challenged rather than accepted at face value.

Work through the design one question at a time:

- ask only one question per turn
- give a recommended answer whenever you can
- keep drilling into unresolved branches until the plan is actionable
- if the answer is already in the repository, inspect the codebase instead of guessing

Keep the tone direct and the questions specific. The goal is to surface missing decisions, hidden assumptions, and implementation risks before work starts.
